Based on innovation

Crazy bakery uses the highest quality ingredients from local producers who are committed to sustainable practices. Energy is expensive both in money terms and environmental impact. Our ovens have been designed by innovation Queensland to having the lowest heat lost and save energy. Although our lighting and cooling are the most efficient available it is still our ovens that use most energy. Our ovens run on ethanol that is sourced from the same farm as our flour, we are still looking at ways to further reduce our impact. Crazy
